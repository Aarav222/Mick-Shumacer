Mick Shumacer

Mick Schumacher is a German racing driver, due to race for Haas in Formula One in 2021, and is also a member of the Ferrari Driver Academy. He began his career in karting in 2008, progressing to the German ADAC Formula 4 by 2015.He recently won the f2 2020 championship by 14 points over Callum Illot.He might even be a future f1 champion.
